About Game Arknights

Arknights is a Chinese mobile tower defense gacha video game developed by Studio Montagne, Hypergryph. It was first released in China on 1 May 2019. Its global server was then released on 16 January 2020 by Yostar. The game is available on iOS and Android platforms
